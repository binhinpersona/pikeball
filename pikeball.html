What is Pikeball?

Pikeball is a fast-paced, team-based sport that combines elements of volleyball, tennis, and four-square. Played on a circular net, it challenges players to showcase their agility, strategy, and teamwork. Perfect for players of all ages, Pikeball is rapidly growing in popularity around the world.

How to Play

  • Each team consists of two players.
  • Teams take turns hitting the ball onto the circular net, aiming to make it difficult for the opposing team to return.
  • A point is scored when the opposing team cannot return the ball properly.
  • First team to reach 21 points wins, but you must win by at least 2 points.

What You Need

To play Pikeball, you will need:

  • A Pikeball set (net and ball).
  • A flat playing surface (indoors or outdoors).
  • Comfortable sportswear and shoes.
